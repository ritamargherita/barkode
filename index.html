Why This Matters

Pet food transparency under EU law

European feed labelling rules are strict, but not every label you see follows them. barkode helps pet owners and professionals quickly spot what's missing, what's questionable, and what would need deeper investigation.

This is a product prototype. It starts with dog food and expands as testing and data coverage grow.

What barkode checks

The prototype focuses on label transparency and core mineral balance.

  • Required label elements under EU law (type, species, feeding guide, additives)
  • Analytical constituents presence and reasonableness
  • Calcium-to-phosphorus ratio for the selected species and life stage
  • Distinction between complete, complementary, and mineral feeds
